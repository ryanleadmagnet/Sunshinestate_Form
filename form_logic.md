# Sunshine State Solar - Form Logic Extraction

This document contains the logic, questions, captions, and answer options extracted from the Heyflow form at [sss-v1#start](https://heathered-boom-highfalutin.heyflow.site/sss-v1#start).

---

## Form Steps

### Step 1: Residency Status
*   **Question:** Do you own your home?
*   **Caption:** Please select the option that best describes your situation:
*   **Options:** 
    *   Yes, I Own
    *   No, I Rent *(Note: This leads to a dead end/disqualification)*

### Step 2: Home Age
*   **Question:** What's the age of your home?
*   **Caption:** Please select the estimated age of your home:
*   **Options:** 
    *   0 - 10 years
    *   10 - 20 years
    *   20+ years

### Step 3: Existing Solar
*   **Question:** Do you have an existing solar system installed?
*   **Caption:** Please indicate whether you already have a solar system installed:
*   **Options:** 
    *   No, I don't
    *   Yes, I do

### Step 4: Shading
*   **Question:** Do you have any shading issues over the roof?
*   **Caption:** Please indicate whether your property has any shading that affects sunlight exposure:
*   **Options:** 
    *   No shading issues
    *   Minor shading issues
    *   Major shading issues
    *   Not sure

### Step 5: Power Usage Pattern
*   **Question:** When do you use most of your power?
*   **Caption:** Please indicate when your household consumes the most electricity:
*   **Options:** 
    *   Mostly day time
    *   Mostly night time
    *   A mixture of both

### Step 6: Average Bill
*   **Question:** How much is your power bill?
*   **Caption:** Please select the average cost your power bill:
*   **Options:** 
    *   $0 - $300
    *   $300 - $600
    *   $600+

### Step 7: Desired System Size
*   **Question:** Which solar system size are you considering?
*   **Caption:** Please select the system size you're interested in:
*   **Options:** 
    *   6.6kW
    *   10.5kW
    *   13.2kW
    *   15.4kW
    *   Not sure

### Step 8: Battery Interest
*   **Question:** Would you like to add a solar battery to your system?
*   **Caption:** Please select if you'd like a solar/battery package:
*   **Options:** 
    *   Yes
    *   No
    *   Not sure

### Step 9: Location
*   **Question:** Please enter your postcode:
*   **Options:** 
    *   [Input Field] Postcode

### Step 10: Name
*   **Question:** What is your name?
*   **Options:** 
    *   [Input Field] First Name
    *   [Input Field] Last Name

### Step 11: Email
*   **Question:** What's your best email?
*   **Caption:** Your email address
*   **Options:** 
    *   [Input Field] Email

### Step 12: Contact Number
*   **Question:** What's your best contact number?
*   **Options:** 
    *   [Selector] Country Code (Default: Australia +61)
    *   [Input Field] Phone Number
    *   [Button] Submit/Get My Quote
